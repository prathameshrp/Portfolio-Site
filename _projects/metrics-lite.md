Metrics Lite is a tiny analytics service for people who want to understand their
traffic without shipping their visitors' data to a third party.

## Highlights

- No cookies, no personal data — GDPR-friendly by design.
- Single binary + SQLite; runs comfortably on a $5 VPS.
- Clean dashboard with the handful of numbers that actually matter.
